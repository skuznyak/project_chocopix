## URL Slug SEO

All URLs must be SEO-friendly.

---

### Rules:

- Use lowercase only
- Use hyphens "-" instead of spaces
- No underscores "_"
- No random IDs or numbers
- Keep URLs short and readable
- Use keywords in slug

---

### Language:

Use transliteration (Latin), not Cyrillic.

Example:
- какао бомбочки з маршмелоу → kakao-bombochky-z-marshmelou

---

### Homepage

/
(no changes)

---

### Category Page

/cacao-bombs

OR

/kakao-bombochky

---

### Product Pages

Format:

/kakao-bombochka-[name]

Examples:

/kakao-bombochka-classic
/kakao-bombochka-marshmelou
/kakao-bombochka-love-set

---

### IMPORTANT

- Slug must match page content
- Do NOT change existing URLs without redirect

---

### Redirects

If slug changes:

- add 301 redirect from old URL to new URL
